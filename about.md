## Who makes this site

This site is researched, written and paid for by one Boyup Brook resident. It is not funded by, affiliated with, or endorsed by the Shire of Boyup Brook or any organisation.

## AI disclosure

This site is built with the assistance of an AI tool — Claude, made by Anthropic. The AI is used for research assistance, drafting, checking documents and building the site's pages.

The difference between using a tool and being used by one is disclosure and verification, so here is how it works on this site: every fact, figure, date and quote is checked against the primary source by me - in person — the Shire's published minutes, agendas, attachments and reports, or the legislation on legislation.wa.gov.au — before it is published. Sources are linked on every page so readers can verify everything independently. The the ai finds the documents and the human author reviews everything and is responsible for all content.

No confidential, personal or sensitive information about any person is entered into the AI tool. The material this site works with is the Shire's own published public record.

The WA Government's AI toolkit for public sector staff suggests that publicly available content created with an AI tool should carry a disclaimer. This page is that disclaimer, for this whole site.

Source: [AI toolkit for WA public sector staff](https://www.wa.gov.au/organisation/department-of-the-premier-and-cabinet/office-of-digital-government/artificial-intelligence-toolkit-wa-public-sector-staff), Office of Digital Government.
